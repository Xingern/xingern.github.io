Key Skills

  • Programming

    Experience with scientific data analysis with Python. Taught students Python as a learning assistant. Familiar with object-oriented programming in both Python and C++.

  • Mathematics

    Taken multiple university-level courses such as calculus, differential equations, numerics, linear algebra, and statistics. Been a learning assistant and have an overall great understanding of mathematics.

  • Chemistry

    Great understanding of the fundamental chemistry courses such as general, inorganic, organic, and physical chemistry. In addition, I have a great understanding of chemical engineering subjects like mass and energy balances, fluids, heat transfer, separation, and reaction engineering.

  • Teamwork

    As an active member of Høiskolens Chemikerforening, I have experience working in teams. When dealing with complicated problems, I approach being structured and systematic. I am fond of autonomous teams where leaders also participate in projects.

  • Communication

    Using my experience in teaching and leadership, I am able to see from multiple perspectives. I enjoy using digital tools to improve communication within, but also between teams.

  • Learning and teaching

    I thrive on improving and learning new skills using online courses and tutorials. My time as a learning assistant and also volunteering has taught me the importance of lifelong learning.
